Who We Are

Rebirth crew is a group formed by youths with hunger for God and the need to affect other youths with this same hunger in their Generation

Vision


The vision of this great house is to awaken the general in every youth (Job 32: 8).

Mission Statement


The overall Mission of Rebirth Crew is to affect youths of this generation positively, showing them the light of God in their everyday life and helping them to know God while nuturing their Talent into success [Matthew 5 : 14-16,John 1 : 4], Also teaching today's youth the very act of birthing other youths into he nature of God they already carry(John 1:4), Leadership and Family Management,Teaching Generations to know God and achieving His purpose on earth, Sponsoring Youths with talents for Trainings and seminars.
